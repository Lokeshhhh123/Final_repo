About Me
Computing is My Passion

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, computing skills have become essential across almost all aspects of life. Whether in education, business, healthcare, or entertainment, proficiency in computing provides numerous benefits and opportunities.

My journey in computing began when I was in high school, where I developed a fascination for how computers work and how they can be secured. This curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Cyber Security at The British College.

Throughout my academic journey, I've gained experience in various programming languages and cybersecurity tools. I enjoy solving complex security challenges and staying updated with the latest trends in the cybersecurity landscape.

Beyond academics, I participate in CTF competitions, attend cybersecurity workshops, and contribute to open-source security projects. These experiences have deepened my understanding of real-world security issues and solutions.
